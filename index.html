Safe space for you to take photos without risking your life

What is safe space? (INITAL RESEARCH)

Safe Space is an app that is useful for travellers to understand the higher chances off risking their lives while they travel around the world taking photos. Safe Space tracks where you are and sensors anything around you to keep you safe. For example if you are taking photos from a great height position or if there is a train behind you it will creates alerts if you are going through a hazard or tells you that you are in danger. It also has emergency numbers that you need.

AIM

To provide an app that helps travellers to be safe when taking photos in dangerous environments. Being able to be legible and easy to use for all people.

TARGET AUDIENCE

The target audience for safe space is for anyone who is travelling and taking photographs in areas that they don't really know. The app is accessible to anyone and also to help make photographers safe while they take photos.

VISUAL DIRECTION

Safe Space will have selective themes. Choosing a theme that suits you. There are two different tones which is the blue and black which can be used for the main UI. As the light grey will show some contrst for the primary elements and aswell as the text elements also with the black for smaller texts.

FONTS

Breeze Sans and Calibri will be used. The decision was clear, as both fonts fit with the hook of outdoor photography. Breeze Sans will serve as the main font-family, used for paragraphs and smaller text elements. Calibri is a very strong bold font, and will be used for larger text elements, such as the temperature or rainfall weather components.
